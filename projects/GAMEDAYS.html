Project overview

In our year, the theme was journalism. Call For Justice is a 2D story-driven adventure made in Unity, where you play an investigative journalist trapped in a web of lies, corruption, and betrayal.

You explore the city, talk to characters, gather clues, and progress through movement on the map, interactions, and short mini-games that push the narrative forward while the mafia closes in.

My contribution

  • Implemented character movement and basic animation logic on the overworld map (controller + Animator).
  • Designed, implemented, and integrated several mini-games used throughout the story progression.
  • Worked with the team on rapid iteration, debugging, and polishing gameplay under strict 48-hour time limits.
