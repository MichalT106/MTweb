Project overview

Call For Justice is a 2D narrative game where the player follows a journalist uncovering corruption.

Progression is driven by exploration, character movement on the map, interactions with NPCs, and simple mini-games.

My contribution

  • Implemented several mini-games used throughout the story progression.
  • Built the character movement system for navigating the map.
  • Helped with quick iteration and integration under strict time limits.

What I gained

  • Collaboration under time pressure and fast decision-making.
  • Effective task division and prioritization in a small team.
  • Hands-on experience with a full development cycle in a very short timeframe.
