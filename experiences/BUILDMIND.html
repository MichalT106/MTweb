Role overview

I work on automated collection of real-estate and market data using UiPath workflows and Python-based scrapers. For more complex or unsupported websites, I implement scraping logic in Python (APIs, requests, BeautifulSoup) and deploy it as AWS Lambda functions, with results integrated into downstream data pipelines.

Key responsibilities

  • Implement new scraping projects and data pipelines for real-estate and market analytics.
  • Maintain and fix existing scrapers when websites change, including selectors, parsing rules, and anti-bot workarounds.
  • Upload and manage UiPath automation workflows in UiPath Orchestrator and run scheduled jobs reliably.
  • Monitor data quality and consistency in the database and correct issues to keep analytics and graphs accurate.
  • Update historical data when required to ensure long-term correctness of reports.
  • Participate in testing of new application releases.

Technologies & tooling
